How to get here

We're located at Bjørnheimveien 4F -an easy-to-find spot in the heart of town. Whether you're driving, biking, or using public transit, getting here is simple:

  • By Car: Free parking is available in our lot off Exploration Street.
  • By Bus: Take the Number 5 or 8 to the Wonderville Science Stop.
  • By Bike: Bike racks are available near the entrance. Plan your journey and enjoy the adventure!

Plan Your Visit

  • Opening Hours: Monday-Saturday: 9 AM - 6 PM; Sunday: 10 AM - 5 PM.
  • Tickets: Buy tickets at the door. 50kr for adults 18 and above. Children free entrance
  • Amenities: Enjoy a meal at our on-site café or pick up souvenirs at the Discovery Shop. Don't forget to check our calendar for upcoming events and special workshops.
