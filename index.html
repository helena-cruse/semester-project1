Community Science Museum

The Community Science Museum is a fun and interactive space designed for children and families. Discover the wonders of science through hands-on exhibits, live demonstrations, and engaging workshops. Perfect for curious minds of all ages, the museum brings science to life in a way that's both educational and entertaining.

What You Can Do at the Museum

Explore, learn, and play! At the Community Science Museum, you can:

  • Experiment with cool gadgets in the innovation Lab.
  • Step into our immersive Planetarium for a journey through the stars.
  • Participate in daily science shows and live experiments.
  • Visit our seasonal exhibits that change every few months! There is always something new to discover.
