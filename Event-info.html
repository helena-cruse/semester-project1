Learn to Grow Bacteria with Agar!

Date: 02.01.2025
Time: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Age Group: 7 - 15 years

In this hands-on activity, children will learn how to grow bacteria on agar plates.

What You'll Do:

  • Prepare agar plates and collect bacteria samples.
  • Inoculate the plates and observe bacterial growth.
  • Learn about microbes, hygiene, and the science behind bacterial cultures.

Educational Goals:

  • Discover the world of microorganisms.
  • Understand the importance of cleanliness.
  • Observe the growth of harmless bacteria.

Let us Have Fun with Liquid Nitrogen!

Date: 02.01.2025
Time: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Age Group: 7 - 15 years

In this hands-on activity, children will safely explore the wonders of liquid nitrogen! They will watch as liquid nitrogen turns into gas, creating cool clouds, and discover how it can freeze things instantly, like flowers or even marshmallows!

What You'll Do:

  • Watch as liquid nitrogen transforms from liquid to gas, creating fog.
  • Freeze objects and see how they shatter or change shape.
  • Learn about the super-cold properties of liquid nitrogen and its uses in science.

Educational Goals:

  • Understand the states of matter and temperature.
  • Explore the science behind extreme cold.
  • Have fun with hands-on demonstrations of cryogenics!
